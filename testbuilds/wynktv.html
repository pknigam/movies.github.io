Wynk TV - iOS

[iOS] How to Share:
  1. Turn on bluetooth or wifi on both devices
  2. Open app on both devices, so that app finds each other
  3. Tap on overflow icon of a downloaded song in Device 1
  4. Select "Share song" action
  5. Select the name of device 2
  6. In device 2 give permission to connect
  7. On transfer complete a toast message will be shown
  8. on Device 2 > Goto screen where this content exists and start playing

[iOS] Note:
  1. On first time app launch, if pop-up is seen “Untrusted Enterprise Developer”, To solve this problem:
  2. Settings App -> General -> Profiles or Device Management
  3. Under ENTERPRISE APP, choose “BSB Portal Ltd.”
  4. Tap Trust "BSB Portal Ltd."
  5. Tap "Trust" in pop up.
  6. Done
  7. Now app can be opened
